**The ultimate open-source streaming platform.** Aggregate streams from your configured sources, manage playback via supported providers, and enjoy on Stremio, Kodi, or directly in your browser.

## What can MediaFusion do?

| | |
|---|---|
| **Rich Catalogs** | Movies, series, live TV, and sports in 50+ languages including Tamil, Hindi, Malayalam, and Kannada |
| **Debrid & P2P** | Real-Debrid, AllDebrid, Premiumize, Torbox, and 8 more providers — or stream directly via P2P |
| **Live TV & Sports** | Formula Racing, UFC/WWE, NFL, NBA, soccer, cricket, and more via configurable sources |
| **Smart Filters** | Sort streams by quality, size, seeders, cached status, audio tracks, and HDR format |
| **Multi-client** | Stremio addon, Kodi addon, in-browser player, Torznab indexer for Radarr/Sonarr |
| **Self-Hosted** | Docker Compose, Kubernetes, or a bare binary — full control over your data |
