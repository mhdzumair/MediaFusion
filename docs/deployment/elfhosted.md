# ElfHosted (Managed)

[ElfHosted](https://elfhosted.com) is a managed PaaS that hosts MediaFusion for you with one-click setup. New accounts get **$10 free credit** to trial the service.

!!! note
    ElfHosted hosts a public community instance at [mediafusion.elfhosted.com](https://mediafusion.elfhosted.com) as a community service. Private instances are separate products and are not affiliated with the MediaFusion project.

## Setup

### 1. Create an ElfHosted account

Go to [store.elfhosted.com/my-account](https://store.elfhosted.com/my-account/) and register. Choose a **lowercase username starting with a letter** — it becomes part of your instance URL.

Confirm your free $10 ElfBuckz at **My Account → Account Funds**.

### 2. Subscribe to MediaFusion

Add [MediaFusion](https://store.elfhosted.com/product/mediafusion) to your cart.

### 3. Optional add-ons

| Add-on | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [RDTClient](https://store.elfhosted.com/product/rdtclient) | Download debrid torrents via Radarr/Sonarr |
| [qBittorrent (BYO VPN)](https://store.elfhosted.com/product-category/download-media/qbittorrent) | Traditional torrent downloading |
| [ElfStorage](https://store.elfhosted.com/product/elfstorage) | Storage for downloaded media (100 GB free, expandable) |

!!! warning "Storage note"
    ElfStorage can be increased in 1 TB chunks but **cannot be shrunk** — plan carefully before adding more.

### 4. Checkout

Check out using your ElfBuckz balance. After payment:

- Your dashboard: `https://<username>.elfhosted.com`
- Your MediaFusion: `https://<username>-mediafusion.elfhosted.com`

### 5. Configure and install

Navigate to your MediaFusion URL, configure the addon (catalogs, providers, filters), and install it into Stremio as you normally would.
